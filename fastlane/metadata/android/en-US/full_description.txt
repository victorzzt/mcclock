MC Clock Widget is an unofficial home screen widget inspired by the Minecraft in-game clock.

It provides eight widget variants, including classic pixel-art styles, newer gold-ingot-style designs, high-resolution retouches by the developer of this app, and moon-phase variants at night-time.

Add the widget from your launcher's widget picker and choose the style you prefer.

The app works fully offline. It does not show ads, does not include telemetry, and does not request special permissions.

This is a fan project. It is not an official Minecraft product and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or approved by Mojang AB or Microsoft.
